POTENTIAL OF THE CAPE GOOSEBERRY IN THE INTERNATIONAL MARKETS

In Colombia, the production of cape gooseberry has had an upward trend due to the opportunities in the international markets. Its high sugar and vitamin content make it attractive to the new green markets, This has led to the second rank of Colombian fruits exports, below banana. Consequently, the production of cape gooseberry began to have multiple benefits, these include exception to tariff tax in commercial agreement with some countries. This has helped to have opening in different destination of green consume. Sold as fresh fruit, snacks, or juice natural, demonstrating its high competitive value in the international market.

The cape gooseberry is characterized by sweet, vitamin, and an exotic fruit. The plant born wild or semi-wild, its production given in grounds between 1500 meters about the level of sea and 2800. In Colombia the production it's in 3 departments, which comply with good conditions for the sow of this plant.
